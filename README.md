---

## 📋 About

Slime Pet Simulator is a desktop virtual pet game where you take care of a digital slime. Its three core stats — **Hunger**, **Energy**, and **Happiness** — decay in real time. You need to feed, let sleep, and play with your pet to keep it healthy. The game also calculates your pet's state while the app is closed, based on how much time has passed.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Real-time stat decay** – Hunger, Energy, and Happiness decrease automatically
- **Offline state calculation** – Pet status updates based on elapsed time since last session
- **Core interactions** – Feed, Sleep, and Play to manage your pet
- **Status conditions** – Pet becomes tired or sick if stats drop too low; cure with rest or medicine
- **Level / Age system** – Track your pet's growth
- **Save & Load** – Game state persists between sessions
- **Visual feedback** – 2D slime sprite with expression changes, progress bars for all stats
- **Text status display** – Current needs shown below the stat bars
- **Main Menu** – Start new game, continue, or exit
- **Settings** – Volume slider (ambient music can be toggled)
- **About screen** – Project info with link to this repository
- **Cross-platform** – Windows and Linux (x86-64)

---

## 🎮 How to Play

| Action | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Feed** | Reduces hunger |
| **Sleep** | Regenerates energy |
| **Play** | Increases happiness |
| **Medicine** | Cures sickness |
| **Rest** | Recovers from fatigue |

Keep an eye on the text under the stat bars — it tells you what your slime needs.
